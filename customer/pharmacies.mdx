## Browse Pharmacies

1. Tap the **Pharmacy** tab (second icon — labeled "Pharmacy")
2. A list of all verified pharmacies appears, sorted by distance from your location
3. Each card shows: logo, store name, rating (stars + review count), address, distance
4. Pharmacies within 10km have a green **NEARBY** badge and a green border
5. Use the search bar at the top (hint: *Search by location or name...*) to filter by name or address

## View a Pharmacy

1. Tap any pharmacy card
2. The pharmacy detail page shows:
   - **Store header** — Logo, name, rating, bookmark icon
   - **Stats bar** — Rating, Reviews count, Orders count
   - **Contact info** — Address, phone, email, working hours
   - **Search bar** — Search within this pharmacy's products
   - **Category filter** — Filter products by category
   - **Product grid** — All available products
3. Actions available:
   - Tap the **phone icon** to call the pharmacy
   - Tap the **share icon** to share the pharmacy profile
   - Tap the **bookmark icon** to save (requires login — see below)
   - Tap the **chat icon** to message the pharmacy (requires login)
   - Tap a product card to see details

## View Product Details

1. Tap any product card (from Home featured, pharmacy page, search results, or categories)
2. The product detail page shows:
   - **Image carousel** — Swipe left/right through photos
   - **Back arrow** (top-left) — Go back
   - **Heart icon** (top-right) — Add to wishlist (requires login)
   - **Product info** — Category badge, brand name, product name, price (ETB), discount if any
   - **Seller card** — Which pharmacy sells this, tap to visit their page
   - **Specifications** — Dosage, Expiry, Pack Size
   - **About Medicine** — Description
   - **Reviews & Ratings** — Average rating, star breakdown, individual reviews with dates
3. Bottom bar has 3 buttons:
   - **Chat icon** (circle, left) — Message the seller (requires login)
   - **Cart icon** (outlined, center) — Add to cart (works as guest)
   - **Buy Now** (filled green, right) — Clears cart, adds this product, goes to checkout (requires login to complete)

## Write a Review

1. On a product detail page, scroll to **Reviews & Ratings**
2. Tap **Write a Review** (requires login)
3. In the dialog, tap 1-5 stars to rate
4. Type your experience in the text field (hint: *Share your experience...*)
5. Tap **Submit**

## Wishlist (Save Products)

1. On any product detail page, tap the **heart icon** (top-right)
2. If not logged in: dialog appears — *"Please log in to save medicines to your wishlist."*
3. If logged in: the heart fills red, product is saved
4. Access your wishlist:
   - From Home tab quick actions — tap **Wishlist**
   - Each product shows with a filled red heart — tap again to remove

## Bookmark a Pharmacy

1. On a pharmacy card (in Pharmacy tab list) or a pharmacy detail page, tap the **bookmark icon**
2. If not logged in: dialog appears — *"Please log in to bookmark your favorite pharmacies."*
3. If logged in: icon changes to filled bookmark
4. Access bookmarks from Home tab quick actions — **Bookmarks** (note: tapping this currently does nothing — feature not fully implemented)

## Chat with a Seller

1. On any product detail page, tap the **chat icon** in the bottom bar
2. If not logged in: red toast — *"Please login to access this feature."*
3. If logged in: opens chat screen with the seller, pre-filled with an inquiry about the product

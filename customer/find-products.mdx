# Find Products

This page shows you how to find the medicines and health products you need.

## Search for a Medicine

1. On the home screen, tap the **search bar** at the top
2. Type the name of the medicine you are looking for
3. Results appear as you type
4. Tap a result to see the product details

> **Screenshot needed:** Search bar with results

## Scan a Barcode

1. On the home screen or search page, tap the **barcode icon**
2. Point your phone camera at the barcode on the medicine package
3. The app will find the product for you instantly

> **Screenshot needed:** Barcode scanner in action

## Browse by Category

1. On the home screen, scroll down to **Categories**
2. You will see icons for different medicine types:
   - Pain relief
   - Antibiotics
   - Vitamins and supplements
   - Cold and flu
   - Allergy
   - Skin care
   - Baby and child health
   - And more
3. Tap a category to see all products in that group

> **Screenshot needed:** Categories section on home screen

## Use Filters

When searching, you can narrow down results:

1. Tap the **filter icon** next to the search bar
2. Choose from these options:
   - **Price range** — Set a minimum and maximum price
   - **In stock only** — Show only products that are available now
   - **Prescription needed** — Show only products that require a prescription
   - **Rating** — Show only products with a certain rating or higher
3. Tap **Apply** to see your filtered results

> **Screenshot needed:** Filter screen with options

## Featured Products

On the home screen, you will see a **Featured** section. These are special products that pharmacies want to promote. They may have discounts or special offers.

## View All Categories

1. On the home screen, tap **See All** next to the Categories section
2. Browse the full list of categories
3. Tap any category to see its products

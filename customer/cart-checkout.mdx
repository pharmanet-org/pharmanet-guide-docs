## Add an Item to Cart

1. Go to any product detail page
2. In the bottom bar, tap the **cart icon** (labeled with a cart symbol)
3. A green toast appears: *"`{product name}` added to cart"*
4. This works even if you're not logged in

## Buy Now (Add and Go to Checkout)

1. On a product detail page, tap **Buy Now** in the bottom bar
2. This clears your cart, adds the single product, and navigates to checkout
3. If not logged in: you'll see a login dialog at checkout

## Open Your Cart

1. Tap the **Cart** tab (third icon — labeled "Cart")

**If you're a guest:**
- You see a person icon, "Login Required" title, and description about signing in
- Tap **Sign In / Create Account** to go to the login screen
- Your cart items are saved locally — they'll still be there after you log in

**If you're signed in:**
- Each cart item shows: product image, name, manufacturer, price (ETB), quantity selector
- Tap **+** or **-** to adjust quantity
- Tap the **trash icon** (top-right of app bar) to clear the entire cart
- A confirmation dialog appears: *"Clear Cart — Are you sure you want to remove all items from your cart?"*
  - Tap **CLEAR ALL** to confirm, or **CANCEL**

## Cart Summary

At the bottom of the cart page:

| Item | Value |
|------|-------|
| **Subtotal** | Sum of all item prices |
| **Delivery Fee** | FREE |
| **Total Amount** | Final total in ETB |

Tap **Proceed to Checkout** to continue.

## Checkout

1. On the checkout page, start with **Shipping Address**:
   - If no address is saved: tap **Add New** to enter one
   - If you have addresses: the selected one is shown with name, address line, city, country
   - Tap the **edit icon** (pencil) to change the address
2. **Order Summary** shows each item with quantity, name, and line total
3. **Upload Prescription** — Only visible if any item requires a prescription:
   - Tap the upload area to select a photo from your gallery
   - The selected image preview appears
4. **Total** shows the final amount in ETB
5. Tap **Pay Now**

## Payment Process

After tapping **Pay Now**:

1. **Not logged in?** A dialog appears: *"Login Required — Please login to access this feature."* Tap **Login** to go sign in
2. **No address selected?** A red toast: *"Please select a shipping address first"*
3. **Prescription required but not uploaded?** A red toast: *"Customers must upload a prescription"*
4. If all checks pass:
   - The order is created
   - The Chapa payment page opens in your browser
   - Complete payment on the Chapa page
   - When payment succeeds, you're returned to the app
   - Your cart is cleared
   - A success message shows: *"Payment confirmed! Order is being prepared."*

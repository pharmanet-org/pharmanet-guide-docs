## Bottom Navigation Bar

The app has 5 tabs at the bottom:

| Icon | Label | Page |
|------|-------|------|
| Home icon | **Home** | Main feed |
| Hospital icon | **Pharmacy** | All pharmacies |
| Shopping bag icon | **Cart** | Your cart |
| Timelapse icon | **Track Orders** | Your orders |
| User icon | **Profile** | Your account |

## Home Tab Layout

From top to bottom:

1. **App bar** — Shows "PharmaNet" title and your current location
2. **Quick actions row** — 6 round icons with labels:
   - **Search** (badge "New") — Opens Advanced Search
   - **Orders** (badge with count) — Opens Orders page
   - **Wishlist** (badge with count) — Opens your saved products
   - **Alerts** (badge with count) — Opens Notifications
   - **Bookmarks** — Does nothing (not yet implemented)
   - **Coupons** — Does nothing (not yet implemented)
3. **Search bar** — Tap to open Advanced Search. Tap the scan icon on the right to scan barcodes
4. **Banners** — Horizontal scrolling promo slides. Tap one to go to the linked product/category
5. **Categories** — Horizontal row of category icons. Tap one to see products in that category. Tap **See All** for the full grid
6. **Featured Products** — Grid of promoted medicines. Tap **See All** for more

## Advanced Search

1. Tap the search bar (hint: *Search for medicines, pharmacies...*) or tap **Search** in quick actions
2. A bottom sheet slides up with these filters:
   - **Search text** — Type a medicine name, brand, or keyword
   - **Category** — Pick a category (default: "All")
   - **Price Range** — Drag the slider (min 10 ETB, max 500 ETB)
   - **In Stock Only** — Toggle on (default: on)
   - **Requires Prescription** — Toggle on/off
   - **Minimum Rating** — Default 4.0, adjust as needed
3. Results appear in a grid below as you type or change filters
4. Tap a product card to view its details

## Scan a Barcode or QR Code

1. On the Home tab, tap the **scan icon** (right side of the search bar)
2. Choose **Medicine Box** (scan barcode/QR) or **Prescription** (scan paper)
3. Point the camera at a barcode or select an image from your gallery

## Browse Categories

1. On the Home tab, scroll to **Categories**
2. Tap any category icon to open a bottom sheet with products in that category
3. Tap **See All** to open the full **All Categories** page with a search bar
4. Tap a category card to filter products by that category

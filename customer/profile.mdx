## Open Your Profile

1. Tap the **Profile** tab (last icon — labeled "Profile")

**If you're a guest:**
- Menu items show a lock icon
- Tap any locked item → dialog: *"Login Required — Please login to access this feature."*
- Tap **Login** to go to the auth screen, or **Cancel**

**If you're signed in:**

Your profile page shows:
- **Profile header** — Avatar, greeting with your name, phone number
- **Order stats grid** — 4 boxes: To Pay, To Ship, Shipped, Delivered (with counts)
- **Action grid** — 4 icons: History, Wishlist (with count badge), Bookmarks, Coupons
- **First Order Deal banner** — "Get 20% OFF on your first prescription."
- **Loyalty Points** card — "You have 250 points to redeem."

## Edit Your Profile

1. On the Profile page, tap **Profile Information**
2. Update any of:
   - **First Name**
   - **Last Name**
   - **Phone Number**
3. Tap the profile image to change it — choose from **Gallery** or **Camera**
4. Tap **Confirm & Save Changes**
5. Green toast: *"Profile updated successfully"*

## Manage Shipping Addresses

1. On the Profile page, tap **Shipping Addresses**
2. **Add a new address:**
   - Tap **Add New**
   - Fill in: full name, phone number, address line 1, city
   - Toggle **Set as default** if this should be your primary address
   - Tap **Save**
3. **Edit an address:** Tap the edit icon next to any address
4. **Delete an address:** Tap the delete icon

## Change Preferred Location (Country)

1. On the Profile page, tap **Preferred Location**
2. Available regions: Ethiopia, Kenya, Djibouti, Sudan, Somalia, US, Canada, UK
3. Tap a country to select it
4. The Home tab header updates to show your selected country flag and name

## Payment Methods

1. On the Profile page, tap **Payment Methods**
2. View saved card details (cardholder name, card number, expiry date)
3. Tap **Add New Payment Card** to add a card (note: this uses mock data — not fully connected to a payment provider)

## Register Your Pharmacy

1. On the Profile page, tap **Register your Pharmacy**
2. Follow the pharmacist registration flow to apply as a seller

## Change Language

1. On the Profile page, tap **Language**
2. A bottom sheet appears with language options
3. Tap **English** or **Amharic**
4. The app UI updates immediately

## Help Center

1. On the Profile page, tap **Help Center**
2. Browse the **documentation links** section with 3 groups:
   - **Guides & Overview** — General app help
   - **Pharmacy (Sellers)** — Registration, Inventory Management, Order Processing, Profile Settings
   - **Dealers (Distributors)** — Account Setup, Listing Products, Manage Orders, Payments, Analytics
3. Below that, browse **Common FAQ** — tap questions to expand answers:
   - *How to place an order?*
   - *What is PharmaNet Points?*
   - *How is my privacy handled?*
   - *Can I cancel an order?*
4. Still need help? Tap **Need Personalized Customer Support?** card to contact support

## Log Out

1. Scroll to the bottom of the Profile page
2. Tap the red **Log Out** button
3. Confirmation dialog: *"Are you sure you want to logout?"*
4. Tap **Log Out** (red) to confirm, or **Cancel**

## First-time Open

1. Install and open the app
2. A white screen with the logo appears while the app loads
3. Then a loading screen with the logo and a green bar appears briefly
4. The app opens directly to the **Home** tab

4 onboarding screens appear on first launch ("Wide Range of Medicines", "Fast & Reliable Delivery", "Upload Prescription Easily", "Health at Your Fingertips"). After swiping through, you start as a guest on the **Home** tab.

## What You Can Do Without Login

- Browse the **Home** tab (banners, categories, featured products, nearby pharmacies)
- Browse the **Pharmacy** tab (all pharmacies, profiles, contact info)
- View **product details** (images, price, brand, specs, description, reviews)
- Use **Search** (text, category, price range, in-stock, prescription, rating)
- Use the **scanner** (barcode/QR code, upload prescription)
- **Add items to cart** (saved on your device)
- Browse **Categories**

## What Requires Login

- **Cart tab** → "Login Required" screen
- **Track Orders tab** → "Sign in / Register" button
- **Profile tab** → lock icons on all items
- **Checkout / Pay Now** → "Please login to access this feature"
- **Wishlist** (heart icon) → "Please log in to save medicines to your wishlist"
- **Bookmark pharmacy** → "Please log in to bookmark your favorite pharmacies"
- **Chat with seller** → "Please login to access this feature"
- **Write a review** → "Login Required" dialog

## After Login (What You Can Now Do)

Once you sign in, the following become available:

| Feature | What changed |
|---------|--------------|
| **Cart tab** | Shows your cart items with quantities, subtotal, free delivery, total. Tap **Proceed to Checkout** to order |
| **Checkout** | Select or add a shipping address, upload prescription if needed, tap **Pay Now** to pay via Chapa |
| **Track Orders tab** | Shows your order list with status badges. Tap **Track Order** for details and timeline |
| **Profile tab** | Shows your name, phone, order stats, action grid (History/Wishlist/Bookmarks/Coupons), Loyalty Points, and all menu items unlocked |
| **Profile Information** | Edit your name, phone number, and profile picture |
| **Shipping Addresses** | Add, edit, or delete delivery addresses |
| **Preferred Location** | Select your country |
| **Payment Methods** | View saved cards |
| **Wishlist** (heart icon) | Saves products. Access from Home tab quick actions or Profile |
| **Bookmark pharmacy** | Saves pharmacies. Access from Home tab quick actions |
| **Chat with seller** | Opens chat screen with pre-filled product inquiry |
| **Write a review** | Rate 1-5 stars and leave a comment on any product |
| **Log Out** button | Appears at the bottom of the Profile page |

## Create an Account (Sign Up)

1. Tap **Sign Up** (the right tab in the toggle)
2. Enter your **Email**
3. Enter a **Password** (minimum 6 characters)
4. Enter **Confirm Password** (must match)
5. Tap the **Sign Up** button
6. On success, you're taken to the main app

## Sign In (Login)

1. Tap **Login** (the left tab in the toggle)
2. Enter your **Email**
3. Enter your **Password**
4. Tap the **Login** button
5. On success, you're taken to the main app

## Sign in with Google or Apple

Below the login form, tap **Google** or **Apple** to sign in with your social account.

## Register as a Pharmacy Seller

Below the social buttons, tap **Are you a Pharmacist? Become a Seller** to start pharmacy registration.

# Manage Your Account

This page shows you how to update your profile, change your preferences, and keep your account secure.

## Your Profile

1. Tap the **Profile** tab at the bottom of the screen
2. You will see:
   - Your **name** and **photo**
   - Your **email** and **phone number**
   - **Order stats** (total orders, completed, pending)

> **Screenshot needed:** Profile page

## Edit Your Profile

1. On your profile page, tap **Edit Profile**
2. You can change:
   - Your **name**
   - Your **profile photo** (take a new photo or choose from your gallery)
   - Your **email address**
   - Your **phone number**
3. Tap **Save** to keep your changes

## Manage Shipping Addresses

1. On your profile page, tap **Shipping Addresses**
2. You will see a list of your saved addresses
3. To add a new address:
   - Tap **Add New Address**
   - Enter the address details
   - Use the **map** to pin your exact location
   - Tap **Save**
4. To edit an address, tap the **pencil icon**
5. To delete an address, tap the **trash icon**

> **Screenshot needed:** Address management screen

## Change Your Language

PharmaNet supports multiple languages:

1. On your profile page, tap **Language**
2. Choose from:
   - **English**
   - **Amharic** (አማርኛ)
   - **Oromo** (Afaan Oromoo)
3. The app will update immediately

> **Screenshot needed:** Language selection screen

## Security Settings

### Change Your Password
1. Go to **Profile** → **Security & Privacy**
2. Tap **Change Password**
3. Enter your **current password**
4. Enter your **new password** (at least 8 characters with a number and capital letter)
5. Enter your **new password again** to confirm
6. Tap **Save**

### Set Up Biometric Login
1. Go to **Profile** → **Security & Privacy**
2. Tap **Biometric Login**
3. Follow your phone's instructions to set up fingerprint or face recognition
4. Once set up, you can log in using your fingerprint or face instead of typing your password

### Turn On Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
1. Go to **Profile** → **Security & Privacy**
2. Tap **Two-Factor Authentication**
3. Follow the instructions to add an extra layer of security to your account

> **Screenshot needed:** Security settings screen

## Notification Settings

1. Go to **Profile** → **Settings**
2. Tap **Notifications**
3. Choose which notifications you want to receive:
   - **Order updates** — get notified when your order status changes
   - **Chat messages** — get notified when a pharmacy replies to you
   - **Promotions** — get notified about special offers
   - **App announcements** — get notified about important updates

## Privacy Settings

1. Go to **Profile** → **Security & Privacy**
2. You can:
   - **Hide order history** — keep your orders private
   - **Data sharing** — control how your data is used
   - **Clear cache** — free up storage space
   - **Clear search history** — remove past searches

## Log Out

1. Go to your **Profile** tab
2. Scroll to the bottom
3. Tap **Log Out**

## Delete Your Account

1. Go to **Profile** → **Security & Privacy**
2. Tap **Delete Account**
3. Confirm your choice
4. Your account and data will be permanently removed

> **Warning:** This action cannot be undone. All your order history and data will be lost.

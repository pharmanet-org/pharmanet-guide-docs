# Place an Order

This page shows you how to order medicines and health products.

## Add a Product to Your Cart

1. Find the product you want (see [Finding Products](/customer/find-products))
2. Tap the product to open its detail page
3. You will see:
   - **Product photos** — swipe to see all images
   - **Price**
   - **Description**
   - **Dosage information**
   - **Manufacturer**
   - **Expiry date**
   - **Stock status** (in stock / low stock / out of stock)
4. Choose the **quantity** using the + and – buttons
5. If the medicine needs a prescription, you will see a note: **Prescription required**
6. Tap **Add to Cart**

> **Screenshot needed:** Product detail page

## Buy Now

If you want to buy a single item quickly:

1. On the product detail page, tap **Buy Now**
2. This takes you directly to checkout with just this item

## View Your Cart

1. Tap the **cart icon** at the bottom of the screen
2. You will see all the items you have added:
   - Product name and photo
   - Quantity (use + and – to change)
   - Price
   - Remove button (trash icon)
3. Below the items, you will see:
   - **Subtotal** (total of all items)
   - **Delivery fee** (if applicable)
   - **Total** to pay

> **Screenshot needed:** Cart page

## Go to Checkout

1. On the cart page, tap **Proceed to Checkout**
2. You will see these steps:

### Step 1: Shipping Address
- Choose an existing address or tap **Add New Address**
- Enter your delivery location (you can use the map to pin your address)
- Add delivery instructions if needed (for example: "Leave at the gate")

### Step 2: Upload Prescription
- If any item needs a prescription, you will be asked to upload one
- Tap **Upload Prescription**
- Take a photo of your prescription or select one from your gallery
- The pharmacy will review it before preparing your order

### Step 3: Order Summary
- Review your items, quantities, and prices
- Check the delivery address
- See the total amount

### Step 4: Payment
- PharmaNet uses **Chapa** for payments
- You can pay with:
  - **Mobile money** (Tele Birr, etc.)
  - **Bank card** (debit or credit)
- Tap **Pay Now**
- Follow the instructions on the Chapa payment screen
- Wait for the payment to be confirmed

> **Screenshot needed:** Checkout screens (address, prescription upload, payment)

## Order Confirmed

Once your payment is successful:

1. You will see a **confirmation message**
2. You will receive a **notification**
3. The pharmacy will start preparing your order
4. You can track your order in the **Orders** tab

> **Tip:** You need to be logged in to place an order. If you are not logged in, you will be asked to log in or sign up before checkout.

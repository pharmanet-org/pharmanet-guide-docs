# Visit Pharmacies

This page shows you how to find pharmacies near you and learn more about them.

## Find Nearby Pharmacies

1. On the home screen, scroll down to **Nearby Pharmacies**
2. You will see a list of pharmacies close to you, sorted by distance
3. Pharmacies that are open now show a **Open** badge
4. Tap **Show Map** to see them on a map

> **Screenshot needed:** Nearby Pharmacies section on home screen

## View Pharmacy Details

1. Tap a pharmacy card to open its detail page
2. You will see:
   - **Pharmacy name** and **photo**
   - **Rating** (stars out of 5)
   - **Distance** from your location
   - **Open or closed** status
   - **Address** and **contact number**
   - **Operating hours** for each day of the week
   - **Location on a map**

> **Screenshot needed:** Pharmacy detail page

## Browse a Pharmacy's Products

On the pharmacy detail page, you can:

1. Scroll down to see their **product catalog**
2. Use the **search bar** to find a specific product in this pharmacy
3. Filter products by **category**
4. Tap any product to see its details and price

## Read and Write Reviews

To read reviews:

1. On the pharmacy detail page, scroll to the **Reviews** section
2. See what other customers say about this pharmacy
3. Each review shows a star rating, comment, and date

To write a review:

1. Tap **Write a Review**
2. Select a **star rating** (1 to 5)
3. Write your **comment**
4. Tap **Submit**

> **Screenshot needed:** Reviews section on pharmacy page

## Bookmark a Pharmacy

If you find a pharmacy you like:

1. Tap the **bookmark icon** (ribbon shape) on the pharmacy page
2. The pharmacy is saved to your **Bookmarks**
3. To see your bookmarked pharmacies, go to your **Profile** tab and tap **Bookmarked Pharmacies**

## Chat with a Pharmacy

From the pharmacy detail page:

1. Tap **Chat**
2. Type your question and send it
3. The pharmacy will reply when they are available

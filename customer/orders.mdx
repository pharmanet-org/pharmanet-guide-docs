## Open Your Orders

There are two ways:

**Method 1 — Track Orders tab:**
1. Tap the **Track Orders** tab (fourth icon — labeled "Track Orders")
2. If not logged in: clock icon, "Track Orders" title, and **Sign in / Register** button
3. If logged in: list of all orders. Tap **Track Order** on any order to see details

**Method 2 — Orders quick action:**
1. On the **Home** tab, tap **Orders** in the quick actions row
2. Opens the **My Orders** page with filter chips

## Filter Orders

On the **My Orders** page, tap a filter chip at the top:

| Chip | What it shows |
|------|---------------|
| **All** | Every order |
| **To Pay** | Orders awaiting payment |
| **To Ship** | Orders being processed |
| **Shipped** | Orders in transit |
| **Delivered** | Completed orders |
| **Cancelled** | Cancelled orders |
| **Returns** | Returned orders |

## Order Cards

Each order shows:

- **Order number** (e.g. `#12345`)
- **Total amount** in ETB
- **Status badge** — colored label at top-right
- **Prescription status** — if applicable (Verification Pending, Approved, or Rejected)
- **View Order Details** button

### Status Badge Colors

| Status | Color |
|--------|-------|
| Pending | Orange |
| Processing / To Ship | Blue |
| Shipped | Green |
| Delivered | Green |
| Cancelled | Red |

## Order Details

1. On any order card, tap **View Order Details** (or **Track Order** on the Track Orders tab)
2. The **Tracking Details** page shows:
   - **Order summary** — Order number, date, total amount, payment method, payment status
   - **Customer info** — Your name and details
   - **Shipping address** — Delivery location
   - **Prescription** — Uploaded image and verification status (if applicable)
   - **Order Items** — Each product with image, name, quantity, and price
   - **Tracking Status** — Timeline of status updates

## Order Lifecycle

1. **Pending** — You placed the order. The pharmacy hasn't confirmed yet
2. **Processing** — The pharmacy confirmed and is preparing your items
3. **Shipped** — Items are on the way to you
4. **Delivered** — You've received the order
5. **Cancelled** — The order was cancelled (before shipping)

## Boost Marketplace Reach

PharmaNet provides a built-in boosting mechanism allowing Sellers and Dealers to pay for increased product visibility on the network.

### Boosting Model

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Paid Visibility" icon="up-long">
    Sellers pay a one-time or subscription fee per product to boost its ranking in the catalog.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Featured Products" icon="star">
    Boosted products appear at the top of search results and on the home screen for increased sales.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Impact on SEO & Sales

- **Network-wide Promotion**: Reach thousands of verified pharmacies and public users.
- **Priority Placement**: Outperform non-boosted items within the same category.
- **Enhanced Credibility**: High-visibility products often attract higher trust from network members.

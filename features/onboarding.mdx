## Verified Strategic Sourcing

PharmaNet ensures every business on the network is legitimate. All Sellers and Dealers must undergo a multi-step verification process.

### Step-by-Step Onboarding

<Steps>
  <Step title="Registration">
    Mobile-first OTP registration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Profile Creation">
    Input store name, slug, address, and bank information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Documentation Upload">
    Upload clear images or PDFs of your **Business License** and **Registration Certificates**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Internal Review">
    Administrator reviews the documentation for authenticity and validity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Platform Activation">
    Receive an automated push notification via **FCM** and start using full features.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Security Features

- **OTP-Locked Profiles**: Ensures that account ownership is tied to a verified mobile number.
- **Admin Moderation**: High-touch verification prevents unverified sellers from entering the pharmaceutical market.
- **Role-Locked Data**: Sellers cannot see other sellers' inventory management tools.

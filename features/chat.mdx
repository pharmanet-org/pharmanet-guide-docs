## Real-time Negotiations

PharmaNet facilitates secure, verified B2B negotiations through an integrated messaging system. All conversations take place between verified business entities.

### Key Capabilities

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Live Messaging" icon="message-dots">
    Negotiate prices, quantities, and delivery schedules in real-time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Document Sharing" icon="file-invoice">
    Securely upload and share **Invoices**, **Payment Proofs**, and **Receipts**.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Order Attachment" icon="link">
    Chat sessions linked to specific product inquiries for full business context.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Read Receipts" icon="check-double">
    Confidence in communication with verified delivery and read statuses.
  </Card>

### Message Integrity & Moderation

- **Verified Senders**: Only approved Sellers, Dealers, and public users can initiate chats.
- **Admin Oversight**: Moderators have access to chat history to resolve disputes and verify business activity.
- **Payment Verification**: Manual upload of payment proof allows for trusted bank-transfer completions before shipment.

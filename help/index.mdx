# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is PharmaNet?
PharmaNet is a platform that connects customers with verified pharmacies. You can search for medicines, compare prices, order online, and get delivery to your door.

### Is PharmaNet free to use?
Yes, the PharmaNet app is free to download and use. You only pay for the medicines and products you order.

### Do I need a prescription to order medicine?
Some medicines require a prescription. The product page will tell you if a prescription is needed. You can upload a photo of your prescription during checkout.

### Which payment methods are accepted?
PharmaNet uses **Chapa** for payments. You can pay with **mobile money** (Tele Birr) or **bank card** (debit or credit).

## Account Questions

### How do I create an account?
Open the app, tap **Sign Up**, enter your details, and verify your phone number with the code sent by SMS. See [Create Your Account](/customer/create-account) for full instructions.

### I forgot my password. What should I do?
On the Log In screen, tap **Forgot Password**. Enter your email and follow the instructions sent to your email.

### Can I use the same account on multiple devices?
Yes, you can log in to your account on any device using your email and password.

### How do I change my language?
Go to your **Profile** tab, tap **Language**, and choose English, Amharic, or Oromo.

## Order Questions

### How long does delivery take?
Delivery time depends on the pharmacy's location and your address. Most orders are delivered within the same day or the next day.

### Can I cancel an order?
Yes, you can cancel an order if it has not been shipped yet. Go to the order detail page and tap **Cancel Order**.

### How do I track my order?
Go to the **Orders** tab and tap the order you want to track. You will see the current status and tracking timeline.

### What if my order arrives damaged?
Contact the pharmacy through chat or contact PharmaNet support. You may be eligible for a return or refund.

### Can I return a product?
Yes, you can return products. Go to the order detail page and tap **Return**. Follow the instructions provided.

## Pharmacy Questions

### How do I know if a pharmacy is verified?
Verified pharmacies show a verification badge on their profile. PharmaNet checks all pharmacies before they can start selling.

### Can I contact a pharmacy before ordering?
Yes, you can chat with any pharmacy by tapping the **Chat** button on their page.

### How do I rate a pharmacy?
After receiving your order, go to the pharmacy's page and scroll to the **Reviews** section. Tap **Write a Review** to leave a rating and comment.

## Technical Questions

### Why am I not receiving the SMS verification code?
Check that you have entered the correct phone number. If you still do not receive the code, try:
- Waiting a few minutes and requesting a new code
- Checking your phone signal
- Contacting support for help

### The app is running slowly. What can I do?
Try these steps:
1. Close the app and open it again
2. Clear the app cache in your phone settings
3. Make sure you have the latest version of the app

### How do I update the app?
Go to the Google Play Store or Apple App Store, search for **PharmaNet**, and tap **Update** if available.

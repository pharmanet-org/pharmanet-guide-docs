# Contact Us

We are here to help. Choose the option that works best for you.

## In-App Support

The fastest way to get help is through the app:

1. Open the PharmaNet app
2. Go to your **Profile** tab
3. Tap **Help Center**
4. Browse the FAQ or tap **Contact Support**
5. Type your question and send it

## Report a Problem

If you find something wrong with the app or a product:

1. Go to **Profile** → **Help Center** → **Report a Problem**
2. Describe the issue
3. Include a screenshot if possible
4. Tap **Submit**

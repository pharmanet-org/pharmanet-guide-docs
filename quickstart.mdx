## Getting Started on PharmaNet

PharmaNet is a closed-loop verified network for pharmaceutical businesses. To begin, follow the registration process that fits your business profile.

### Registration Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download and Install">
    Available as a **Flutter Mobile App** for Sellers and Public users, and a **Next.js Web App** for Administrators and Dealers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="OTP Authentication">
    Register using your mobile number. A secure OTP will be sent via SMS to verify your digital identity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Business Profile Setup">
    If registering as a **Seller (Pharmacy)** or **Dealer (Distributor)**, you'll need to upload:
    - **Business Registration Number**
    - **Valid Business License (Photo/PDF Upload)**
    - **Tax Identification Number (TIN)**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Administrative Approval">
    Our platform moderators will verify your license. While pending, you can browse public inventories but cannot participate in negotiations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Active Trading">
    Once approved, you'll receive a push notification through **Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)**. You can now publish your stock, start conversations, and boost products.
  </Step>
</Steps>

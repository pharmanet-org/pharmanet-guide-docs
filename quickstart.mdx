# Quick Start Guide

Follow these 5 steps to start using PharmaNet.

## Step 1: Download the App

- Open the **Google Play Store** (Android) or **Apple App Store** (iPhone)
- Search for **PharmaNet**
- Tap **Install**

## Step 2: Create Your Account

1. Open the PharmaNet app
2. Tap **Sign Up**
3. Enter your name, phone number, email, and create a password
4. You will receive a **6-digit code** by SMS — enter it to verify your phone
5. Choose **Customer** as your account type

> Already have an account? Tap **Log In** and enter your email and password.

## Step 3: Find What You Need

1. On the home screen, type a medicine name in the **search bar**
2. You can also **scan a barcode** using your phone camera
3. Browse by **category** — pain relief, vitamins, antibiotics, and more
4. Use **filters** to narrow by price, brand, or prescription requirement

## Step 4: Place Your Order

1. Tap a product to see details and price
2. Choose the **quantity** you need
3. Tap **Add to Cart** or **Buy Now**
4. When you are ready, go to your cart and tap **Checkout**
5. Enter your **delivery address**
6. If the medicine needs a prescription, **upload a photo** of your prescription
7. Pay using **Chapa** (mobile money or card)
8. Confirm your order

## Step 5: Receive Your Medicine

1. The pharmacy will prepare your order
2. You will get a **notification** when the order status changes
3. Track your order in the **Orders** tab
4. When it arrives, check your items and confirm delivery

> **Tip:** You can chat with the pharmacy at any time by tapping the **Chat** button on the order page.

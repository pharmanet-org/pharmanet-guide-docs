# Welcome to PharmaNet

PharmaNet connects you with verified pharmacies in your area. You can search for medicines, compare prices, place orders, and get them delivered to your door.

## What You Can Do on PharmaNet

- **Find medicines** — Search by name, category, or scan a barcode
- **Compare pharmacies** — See prices, ratings, and distances
- **Order online** — Add to cart, upload prescriptions, pay with Chapa
- **Track delivery** — Follow your order from the pharmacy to your door
- **Chat with pharmacies** — Ask questions before you buy
- **Save favorites** — Keep a wishlist and bookmark your go-to pharmacies

## Who Uses PharmaNet

| You are a... | You can... |
|---|---|
| **Customer** | Browse medicines, place orders, track deliveries, chat with pharmacies |
| **Pharmacy owner** | List your products, manage orders, track sales, grow your business |

## Download the App

PharmaNet is a mobile app available on:

- **Google Play Store** (Android)
- **Apple App Store** (iPhone)

You can also use the **PharmaNet Seller Dashboard** on your computer to manage your pharmacy.

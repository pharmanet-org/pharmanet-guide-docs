## Elevating Pharmaceutical Supply Chains

PharmaNet is a mission-driven platform designed to resolve fragmented supply chains in emerging markets. We bridge the gap between **Pharmacies (Sellers)** and **Distributors (Dealers)** by providing a unified, verified network for real-time inventory sharing, secure negotiations, and verified business transactions.

Welcome to the PharmaNet Seller Guide. This section helps pharmacy owners manage their business on the platform.

## Two Ways to Manage Your Pharmacy

You can use PharmaNet in two ways:

1. **PharmaNet mobile app** — manage orders, chat, and quick updates on your phone
2. **PharmaNet Seller Dashboard** — full management on your computer with charts, reports, and more

> **New here?** Start with [Register Your Pharmacy](/seller/register).

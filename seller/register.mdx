# Register Your Pharmacy

This page shows you how to create your pharmacy account and get approved.

## What You Need

Before you start, make sure you have:

- A valid **pharmacy license**
- Your **TIN (Tax Identification Number)**
- A **business bank account** in your pharmacy's name
- A **phone number** and **email address**
- Photos of your **pharmacy** (storefront, interior, products)

## Step 1: Sign Up

1. Download the PharmaNet app from Google Play Store or Apple App Store
2. Open the app and tap **Sign Up**
3. Enter your **name**, **email**, **phone number**, and create a **password**
4. Verify your phone number with the **6-digit code** sent by SMS
5. When asked to choose your account type, select **Pharmacy**

## Step 2: Complete Your Pharmacy Profile

Fill in your pharmacy details:

1. **Pharmacy name** — the name of your business as it appears on your license
2. **License number** — your official pharmacy license number
3. **TIN number** — your tax identification number
4. **Phone number** — the pharmacy's contact number
5. **Email address** — the pharmacy's email
6. **Address** — your physical location (use the map to pin it)
7. **Operating hours** — what time you open and close each day

## Step 3: Upload Documents

You need to upload:

1. **Pharmacy license** — a photo or scan of your license document
2. **TIN certificate** — your tax registration certificate
3. **Professional license** — the pharmacist's license
4. **Bank details** — your pharmacy bank account information

Tap **Upload** for each document and select the image from your phone.

## Step 4: Wait for Approval

1. After submitting, your application will be reviewed by the PharmaNet team
2. This usually takes **1 to 2 business days**
3. You will receive a **notification** when your account is approved
4. If more information is needed, you will be contacted

> **Screenshot needed:** Registration form with document upload

## Step 5: Log In and Start Selling

Once approved:

1. Open the PharmaNet app or visit the **Seller Dashboard** on your computer
2. Log in with your email and password
3. You will see your **Dashboard** with an overview of your pharmacy
4. Start by adding your products (see [Manage Products](/seller/manage-products))

> **Tip:** While waiting for approval, prepare your product list and photos so you can start selling right away.

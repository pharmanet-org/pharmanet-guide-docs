# Payments

This page shows you how to manage payments and subscriptions for your pharmacy.

## Payment History

1. Log in to the **Seller Dashboard** on your computer
2. Click **Payments** in the left menu
3. You will see a list of all your payments:
   - **Date** — when the payment was made
   - **Type** — what the payment was for
   - **Amount** — how much was paid
   - **Status** — completed, pending, or failed

> **Screenshot needed:** Payment history page

## How Payments Work

When a customer places an order:
1. The customer pays using **Chapa** (mobile money or card)
2. The payment is processed and held securely
3. When the order is delivered, the payment is released to your account

## Payment Methods

You can receive payments through:
- **Mobile money** (Tele Birr and other services)
- **Bank transfer** — funds are sent to your registered bank account

## Set Up Your Payment Details

1. Go to **Settings** → **Payment Info**
2. Enter your **bank account details**:
   - Bank name
   - Account number
   - Account holder name
3. Enter your **mobile money details**:
   - Mobile money provider
   - Phone number
4. Tap **Save**

> **Screenshot needed:** Payment settings

## Subscription Plans

PharmaNet offers subscription plans for pharmacies that want extra features.

| Plan | Price | Benefits |
|---|---|---|
| **Monthly** | 500 ETB per month | All basic features |
| **Yearly** | 5,000 ETB per year | All features + 2 months free |

### Subscribe or Change Your Plan

1. Go to **Payments** → **Subscription**
2. See your current plan
3. Tap **Change Plan** to upgrade or downgrade
4. Choose the plan you want
5. Follow the payment instructions to complete

> **Screenshot needed:** Subscription page

## View Payment for Featured Products

When you pay for featured products (promotions), the payment appears in your history under **Featured Product Payment**.

See [Promotions](/seller/promotions) for more details on featured products.

> **Tip:** Keep your payment information up to date so there are no interruptions to your service.

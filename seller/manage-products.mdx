# Manage Products

This page shows you how to manage your products on PharmaNet.

## View Your Products

**On mobile:**
1. Open the PharmaNet app and log in with your pharmacy account
2. Tap the **Products** tab or **Inventory** from your dashboard
3. You will see a list of all your products
4. Use the **search bar** to find a specific product
5. Use **filters** to sort by category or stock status

**On the web dashboard:**
1. Log in to the Seller Dashboard on your computer
2. Click **Products** in the left menu
3. You will see your full product list with search and filter options

> **Screenshot needed:** Product list view

## Add a New Product

1. Tap or click **Add Product**
2. Fill in the product details:

| Field | What to Enter |
|---|---|
| **Product name** | The medicine or product name |
| **Description** | A short description (what it is, what it does) |
| **Category** | Choose from the list (pain relief, antibiotics, etc.) |
| **Price** | How much the customer pays |
| **Stock quantity** | How many units you have |
| **Dosage** | The strength or dosage (for example: 500mg) |
| **Manufacturer** | Who makes this product |
| **Expiry date** | When the product expires |
| **Prescription required** | Turn on if customers need a prescription |
| **Photos** | Upload up to 5 photos of the product |

3. Tap **Save** or **Add Product**

> **Screenshot needed:** Add product form

## Edit a Product

1. Find the product in your list
2. Tap or click the **pencil icon** (edit)
3. Change any of the details
4. Tap **Save** to keep your changes

## Delete a Product

1. Find the product in your list
2. Tap or click the **trash icon** (delete)
3. Confirm that you want to delete it
4. The product will be removed from your catalog and customers will no longer see it

## Stock Status Colors

Each product shows its stock status:

- **Green** — In stock (enough quantity)
- **Yellow** — Low stock (running low, order more soon)
- **Red** — Out of stock (none available)

## Low Stock Alerts

When a product runs low:
1. You will see a **notification** on your dashboard
2. The product will show a **yellow** stock indicator
3. Order more stock from your suppliers before it runs out

> **Tip:** Keep your stock information up to date. Customers rely on accurate stock counts when placing orders.

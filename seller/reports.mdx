# Reports

This page shows you how to view reports and understand your pharmacy's performance.

## View Your Reports

Reports are available on the **Seller Dashboard** on your computer:

1. Log in to the Seller Dashboard
2. Click **Reports** in the left menu

You will see:

### Sales Overview
- **Total sales** — how much you have earned
- **Number of orders** — how many orders you have received
- **Average order value** — how much customers spend on average
- **Sales this week / this month / this year** — filter by time period

### Charts
- **Sales trend** — a line chart showing your sales over time
- You can view by **day**, **week**, or **month**
- Hover over the chart to see exact numbers

> **Screenshot needed:** Reports page with charts

### Top Selling Products
- A list of your best-selling products
- Ranked by number of orders
- See which products make you the most money

### Orders Summary
- Total orders received
- Orders by status (pending, processing, shipped, delivered, cancelled)
- See how many orders you completed successfully

## Export Reports

You can download your reports:

1. On the Reports page, click **Export**
2. Choose your format:
   - **PDF** — a document you can print or share
   - **CSV** — a spreadsheet you can open in Excel or Google Sheets
3. Choose the **time period** (this week, this month, custom dates)
4. Click **Download**

> **Screenshot needed:** Export dialog

## Why Reports Matter

Reports help you:
- See which products sell best — stock more of them
- Spot slow periods — plan promotions
- Track your growth — see if your business is improving
- Make better decisions — know what to order and when

> **Tip:** Check your reports weekly to stay on top of your business.

# Promotions

This page shows you how to promote your products and grow your sales.

## Featured Products

Featured products appear in a special section on the home screen that all customers see. This gives your products more visibility.

### How It Works

1. Choose a product you want to promote
2. Pay a **featured fee** of **100 ETB** per product
3. Your product appears in the **Featured** section for a set period
4. Customers see your product before regular search results

### Feature a Product

**On the web dashboard:**
1. Log in to the Seller Dashboard
2. Click **Promotions** in the left menu
3. Click **Add Featured Product**
4. Choose the product from your inventory
5. Confirm the payment (100 ETB)
6. Complete the payment through **Chapa**
7. Your product is now featured

**On mobile:**
1. Open the PharmaNet app with your pharmacy account
2. Go to your **Products** list
3. Tap the product you want to feature
4. Tap **Boost** or **Feature**
5. Follow the payment steps

### View Your Featured Products

1. Go to **Promotions**
2. You will see a list of your currently featured products
3. Each shows:
   - Product name
   - Date it was featured
   - How long it will be featured
   - Number of views or clicks (if available)

## Product Boosting

Product boosting is similar to featuring but gives your product priority in **search results**.

When a customer searches for a medicine, boosted products appear higher in the list.

### Boost a Product

1. Go to **Promotions** → **Boost Product**
2. Select the product
3. Choose the boost duration:
   - **One-time boost** — appears at the top for a day
   - **Subscription boost** — appears at the top for a month
4. Complete the payment
5. Your product is now boosted

## Tips for Promotions

- **Feature your best sellers** — promote products that already sell well
- **Feature new products** — help customers discover them
- **Feature seasonal products** — cold and flu medicines during flu season
- **Use clear photos** — good product photos attract more customers
- **Set competitive prices** — featured products are seen by more customers

> **Tip:** Promotions are a great way to stand out from other pharmacies and attract new customers.

# Account Settings

This page shows you how to manage your pharmacy account settings.

## Access Settings

**On mobile:**
1. Open the PharmaNet app with your pharmacy account
2. Tap the **Profile** tab
3. Tap **Settings**

**On the web dashboard:**
1. Log in to the Seller Dashboard
2. Click **Settings** in the left menu

## Edit Your Personal Information

1. Go to **Settings** → **Personal Info**
2. You can update:
   - Your **name**
   - Your **email address**
   - Your **phone number**
   - Your **profile photo**
3. Tap **Save** to keep your changes

## Edit Your Pharmacy Information

1. Go to **Settings** → **Pharmacy Info**
2. You can update:
   - **Pharmacy name** — your business name
   - **Store slug** — your pharmacy's web address (for example: your-pharmacy-name)
   - **Address** — your physical location (use the map to adjust)
   - **Phone number** — your pharmacy's contact number
   - **Operating hours** — what time you open and close each day
   - **Photos** — update your pharmacy photos

## Update Payment Information

1. Go to **Settings** → **Payment Info**
2. Update your **bank account details** or **mobile money details**
3. Tap **Save**

See [Payments](/seller/payments) for more details.

## Notification Preferences

1. Go to **Settings** → **Notifications**
2. Choose what notifications you receive:
   - **New orders** — get notified when a customer places an order
   - **Chat messages** — get notified when a customer sends a message
   - **Order updates** — get notified when an order status changes
   - **Payment confirmations** — get notified when payments are processed
   - **Promotions** — get notified about promotion opportunities

## Dark Mode

1. Go to **Settings** → **Appearance**
2. Turn **Dark Mode** on or off
3. Dark mode changes the app to a darker color scheme that is easier on the eyes at night

## Switch to Customer View

On the mobile app, you can switch to see PharmaNet as a customer would:

1. In **Settings**, tap **Switch to Customer View**
2. You can browse products and see what customers experience
3. To switch back, tap **Switch to Pharmacy View**

## Log Out

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Scroll to the bottom
3. Tap **Log Out**

> **Tip:** Keep your pharmacy information up to date so customers can find you and contact you easily.

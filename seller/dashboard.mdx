# Dashboard

Your dashboard shows you everything about your pharmacy at a glance.

## Mobile App Dashboard

1. Open the PharmaNet app and log in with your pharmacy account
2. You will see your dashboard with:

### Stats Cards
At the top, you will see numbers that update in real time:

- **Total Sales** — how much you have earned
- **Pending Orders** — orders waiting for your action
- **Total Medications** — how many products you have listed
- **Low Stock Items** — products running low that need restocking

### Charts
- **Weekly Sales Trend** — a line chart showing your sales over the past week
- **Top Performing Products** — your best-selling medicines

### Recent Orders
- A list of your most recent orders
- Tap any order to see details and process it

### Low Stock Alerts
- Products that are running low are highlighted in **yellow** (low stock) or **red** (out of stock)

## Web Dashboard (Seller Dashboard)

The web dashboard on your computer gives you more detailed information:

1. Go to the PharmaNet Seller Dashboard website
2. Log in with your pharmacy email and password

You will see:

### Navigation Menu
On the left side, a menu with these options:
- **Dashboard** — the main overview
- **Products** — manage your product list
- **Orders** — view and process orders
- **Reports** — detailed sales reports
- **Payments** — payment history
- **Promotions** — promote your products
- **Settings** — your pharmacy settings

### Dashboard Overview
- **Stats cards** — same as the mobile app
- **Sales chart** — a bigger, interactive chart
- **Recent orders** — with action buttons
- **Top products** — ranked by sales
- **Inventory alerts** — low stock warnings

> **Screenshot needed:** Web dashboard overview

## Refreshing Your Dashboard

- On mobile: **Pull down** on the screen to refresh
- On web: The dashboard updates automatically, or click the **Refresh** button

## Open Chats

1. In the **Pharmacist Dashboard**, tap the **Chats** tab (fourth icon — message icon)
2. The chat inbox opens with these sections:

### Active Now
A horizontal scrolling row showing customer avatars. A green dot indicates they're online. Your avatar appears first.

### PharmaNet Support Pinned
A pinned card at the top for PharmaNet support (coming soon).

### Recent Messages
A list of all active conversations. Each chat tile shows:

- **Customer avatar** — photo or user icon placeholder
- **Online indicator** — green dot if the customer is currently active
- **Customer name** — their full name
- **Last message** — preview of the most recent message
- **Time** — timestamp of the last message
- **Unread badge** — blue circle with count of unread messages
- **More options** — three-dot menu

## Search Messages

Use the search bar at the top to filter conversations by message content.

## Open a Conversation

Tap any chat tile to open the conversation. The chat screen shows:

### App Bar
- Back button
- Customer avatar and name
- Status label: *"Customer Inquiry"*
- Info icon (non-functional)

### Product Banner (if applicable)
If the chat started from a product inquiry, a blue gradient banner shows:
- Product thumbnail
- Label: *"Inquiry about product"*
- Product name

### Message Thread
- Messages appear as chat bubbles
- **Your messages** — blue gradient bubbles on the right
- **Customer messages** — white bubbles on the left
- **Date headers** — appear between messages on different days (e.g., "January 15, 2026")
- **Image messages** — shown as image thumbnails with tap to view
- **Read receipts** — check icon appears on your sent messages

### Send a Message
1. Type your message in the text field at the bottom
2. Press the **Send** button (blue circle, right) or tap Enter on your keyboard
3. The message appears immediately in the thread

### Send an Image
1. Tap the **+** button (left of the text field)
2. Choose **Camera** to take a photo or **Gallery** to select one
3. The image uploads and appears in the chat

## Chat Options

On the chats list, **long-press** a chat tile or tap the **three-dot menu** to:

| Option | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Clear Chat History** | Deletes all messages in this conversation (confirmation required) |
| **Delete Entire Chat** | Permanently removes the chat for all parties (confirmation required) |

## Chat Settings

Tap the **settings gear** icon in the Chats tab app bar to open Chat Settings:

| Section | Options |
|---------|---------|
| **Notifications** | Push Notifications toggle, Message Sounds toggle |
| **Privacy & Safety** | Read Receipts toggle, Online Status toggle |
| **Data & Storage** | Media Auto-Download toggle, Clear All Chats |

> Note: Most chat settings toggles are UI previews and not yet functional.

## How Customers Start a Chat

Customers can message you from:
- A **product detail page** — tap the chat icon to inquire about a product
- A **pharmacy detail page** — tap the chat icon to message your pharmacy

When they send a message, it appears in your Chats tab automatically.

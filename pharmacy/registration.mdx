# Pharmacist Registration & Login

This guide walks you through creating a pharmacist account and logging into PharmaNet. Follow these steps to start managing your pharmacy inventory and orders.

---

## 📱 Before You Begin

**What you need:**
- A valid **email address** (professional email recommended)
- Your **pharmacy business license** (digital copy or photo)
- Stable internet connection
- PharmaNet app installed on your device

**Download the app:**
- [Download for Android (APK)](https://github.com/pharmanet-org/pharmanet/releases)
- Coming soon to Google Play Store

---

## 1️⃣ Create a Pharmacist Account

### Step 1: Launch the App
Open PharmaNet on your device. You will see the welcome screen.

### Step 2: Tap "Sign Up"
Look for the **"Sign Up"** or **"Create Account"** button at the bottom of the screen.

### Step 3: Select "Pharmacist" Role
Choose **"Pharmacist"** from the list of user roles:
- ❌ Customer
- ❌ Dealer
- ✅ **Pharmacist**

### Step 4: Fill in Your Information

| Field | What to Enter |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Full Name** | Your legal name |
| **Email** | Professional email address |
| **Phone Number** | Active mobile number |
| **Password** | Minimum 8 characters |
| **Confirm Password** | Re-enter the same password |

### Step 5: Enter Pharmacy Details

| Field | What to Enter |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Pharmacy Name** | Registered business name |
| **License Number** | Your official pharmacy license number |
| **Business License File** | Upload a photo or PDF of your license |
| **Address** | Full physical address of the pharmacy |
| **City / Region** | Location details |

### Step 6: Submit for Review
Tap the **"Submit"** or **"Register"** button.

> ⏳ **Note:** Your account will be marked as `pending` until an admin reviews and approves your license. This usually takes 24-48 hours.

---

## 2️⃣ Login to Your Account

### Once Your Account is Approved

You will receive an email notification when your account is approved.

### Step 1: Open the App
Launch PharmaNet on your device.

### Step 2: Tap "Login"
Select the **"Login"** button on the welcome screen.

### Step 3: Choose Login Method

| Method | Instructions |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Email & Password** | Enter your registered email and password, then tap "Login" |
| **Google Sign-In** | Tap the Google button and select your account (if enabled) |
| **Apple Sign-In** | Available for iOS users |

### Step 4: Complete Login
After successful login, you will be redirected to your **Pharmacist Dashboard**.

---

## 3️⃣ Understanding the Pharmacist Dashboard

Once logged in, you will see:

| Section | What You Can Do |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Inventory** | View and manage medicine stock |
| **Orders** | Process incoming customer orders |
| **Profile** | Update pharmacy information |
| **Analytics** | View sales and performance metrics |

---

## 4️⃣ Common Login Issues & Solutions

| Issue | Solution |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Forgot password** | Tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen and follow the email reset link |
| **Account pending** | Wait for admin approval (check email for status) |
| **Invalid credentials** | Double-check email and password, then try again |
| **Google login fails** | Use email/password method instead (Google may not be enabled yet) |
| **"Failed to load pharmacy details"** | This is a known bug. Your dashboard may still work. Contact support if issue persists |

---

## 5️⃣ Updating Your Pharmacy Profile

After login, you can update your information:

1. Go to **Profile** (bottom navigation bar or settings icon)
2. Tap **"Edit Profile"**
3. Update fields as needed:
   - Pharmacy address
   - Phone number
   - Business hours
4. Tap **"Save Changes"**

> ⚠️ **Note:** Email changes may require re-verification.

## Before You Begin

**What you need:**
- A valid email address
- Your **pharmacy business license** 
- Your **pharmacist license** 
-  PharmaNet app installed

---

## 1. Sign Up for a User Account

First, create a customer account:

1. Open the app. Tap the **Profile** tab (bottom-right, user icon)
2. If you're a guest, tap **Sign In / Register**
3. In the auth screen, tap **Sign Up** (the right tab)
4. Enter your **Email** and **Password** (minimum 6 characters)
5. Tap **Sign Up**
6. On success, you're logged into the app as a regular user

## 2. Start Pharmacy Registration

1. Go to the auth screen (you can access it from Profile tab)
2. Below the social sign-in buttons (Google / Apple), tap **Are you a Pharmacist? Become a Seller**
3. This opens the **Pharmacy Registration** form

## 3. Fill Out the Registration Form

### Pharmacy Profile

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Pharmacy Name** | Your registered business name |
| **Pharmacy Address** | Start typing for location suggestions. Tap a suggestion to auto-fill and capture coordinates |
| **Business Phone** | Active contact number |
| **Business Email** | Professional email address |
| **Pharmacy License Number** | Official license number |
| **Description** | Brief about your pharmacy |

### Bank Account Details

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Bank Name** | e.g., Commercial Bank of Ethiopia |
| **Account Name** | Account holder's name |
| **Account Number** | Your bank account number |

### Required Documents

| Document | Required? |
|----------|-----------|
| **Pharmacy License** | Yes — tap to upload a photo |
| **Pharmacist License** | Yes — tap to upload a photo |
| **Pharmacy Logo** | No — optional |

After uploading, you can tap to **preview** the image or **remove** and re-upload.

## 4. Submit

1. Tap the **Sign Up** button at the bottom
2. A success dialog appears: *"Registration Successful"* with a message about pending review
3. Tap **OK** to return to the main app

> After submission, your pharmacy account is marked as `pending`. An admin reviews your documents and approves or rejects your application.

---

## 5. Accessing the Pharmacist Dashboard

Pharmacist role routing in the app is still being finalized. Currently, pharmacists access their dashboard through the **Profile** page:

1. Tap the **Profile** tab (bottom-right, user icon)
2. Look for the **Check Account Status** tile (if pending/rejected) or **Pharmacist Dashboard** tile (if approved)
3. Tap it to navigate to the pharmacist panel

### Status Pages

Depending on your approval status, tapping the tile shows:

| Status | What you see |
|--------|--------------|
| **Approved** | Pharmacist Dashboard with 5 tabs: Home, Inventory, Track, Chats, Profile |
| **Pending** | Pending page with clock icon and "Verification in Progress" message |
| **Rejected** | Rejection screen with the reason provided by the admin |

Contact support if the dashboard option doesn't appear after approval.

---

## Dashboard Overview

| Tab | Icon | What you can do |
|-----|------|-----------------|
| **Home** | Grid | View stats (Total Sales, Pending Orders, Total Meds, Low Stock), sales chart, top products, recent orders, low stock alerts |
| **Inventory** | Box | Add, edit, search, and delete medications |
| **Track** | Receipt | View and process customer orders with status filters |
| **Chats** | Message | Communicate with customers in real-time |
| **Profile** | User | Manage pharmacy info, personal details, security, and help |

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Can't find "Become a Seller" | Scroll down below the Google/Apple buttons on the auth page |
| License upload fails | Make sure the image is clear and under 5MB |
| Both license uploads required | The form won't submit unless both pharmacy and pharmacist licenses are uploaded |
| Registration succeeds but nothing changes | Your account needs admin approval. Wait for your status to change to "approved" |
| Dashboard not showing after approval | Contact support — pharmacist routing is being finalized |

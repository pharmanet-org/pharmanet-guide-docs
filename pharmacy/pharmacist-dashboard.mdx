The **Pharmacist Dashboard** is your main hub for managing your pharmacy. To access it, tap the **Profile** tab, then tap **Pharmacist Dashboard** (shows only for approved pharmacists).

## Navigation

The dashboard has 5 bottom tabs:

| Tab | Icon | Label | Page |
|-----|------|-------|------|
| **Home** | Grid | Home | Main dashboard |
| **Inventory** | Box | Inventory | Medication management |
| **Track** | Receipt | Track | Order management |
| **Chats** | Message | Chats | Customer communication |
| **Profile** | User | Profile | Account settings |

## Home Tab Layout

The Home tab header shows **"Pharmacist Dashboard"** with two icons on the right (currently non-functional):
- **Notification bell**
- **Settings gear**

### Welcome Section

Displays: *"Welcome back, {'{your first name}'}!"*

### Stats Grid (2x2)

| Card | Icon | Shows |
|------|------|-------|
| **Total Sales** | Wallet | Total revenue in ETB |
| **Pending Orders** | Receipt | Number of unprocessed orders |
| **Total Medications** | Box | Total products in your catalog |
| **Low Stock** | Warning | Count of items at or below threshold |

### Weekly Sales Trend

A line chart showing your sales for each day of the week (Mon–Sun). Only appears if sales data exists.

### Top Performing Products

Lists your best-selling products. Each item shows:
- Product thumbnail image
- Product name
- Units sold (e.g., "24 units sold")
- Price in ETB

### Recent Prescription Orders

Shows the 3 most recent orders. Tap **See All** to jump to the Track tab. Each item shows:
- Order number (e.g., `#12345`)
- Total amount (ETB)
- Status badge (color-coded)

### Low Stock Alert

Lists medications running low on stock (at or below threshold, default: 5 units). Tap **See All** to jump to Inventory. Each item shows:
- Product thumbnail
- Product name
- Stock count (e.g., "Only 3 left")

### Pull to Refresh

Pull down on the Home tab to reload all dashboard data.

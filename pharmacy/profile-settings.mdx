# ⚙️ Manage Your Pharmacy Profile

Keep your pharmacy information accurate and up to date. This guide shows you how to edit your profile, change settings, and manage your account.

---

## 🖥️ Where to Find Profile Settings

After logging into your **Pharmacist Dashboard**:

1. Look at the **bottom navigation bar** or **side menu**
2. Tap on **"Profile"** or **"Settings"** (person icon)
3. You will see your pharmacy information screen

---

## 📝 What You Can Update

| Section | What You Can Change |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Pharmacy Info** | Name, address, phone number, email |
| **Business Hours** | Opening and closing times |
| **Payment Settings** | Bank account for payouts |
| **Security** | Password, two-factor authentication |
| **Notifications** | Email and push alert preferences |

---

## ✏️ Edit Pharmacy Information

### Step 1: Tap "Edit Profile"
Look for the **pencil icon** ✏️ or **"Edit"** button at the top right.

### Step 2: Update Your Details

| Field | What to Do |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Pharmacy Name** | Type the registered business name |
| **Address** | Full street address, city, region |
| **Phone Number** | Active contact number |
| **Email** | Professional email address |
| **License Number** | Your official pharmacy license (read-only, contact admin to change) |

### Step 3: Save Changes
Tap **"Save Changes"** at the bottom of the screen.

> ✅ You will see a success message: "Profile updated successfully"

---

## 🕐 Set Business Hours

Help customers know when you are open:

1. Go to **Profile** → **"Business Hours"**
2. Toggle **"Use Custom Hours"** ON
3. Set hours for each day:

| Day | Example |
| :--- | :--- |
| Monday - Friday | 8:00 AM → 10:00 PM |
| Saturday | 9:00 AM → 8:00 PM |
| Sunday | 10:00 AM → 6:00 PM |

4. Tap **"Save"**

> 💡 Customers see your open/closed status when placing orders.

---

## 💳 Update Payment Settings

To receive payouts from orders:

1. Go to **Profile** → **"Payment Settings"**
2. Enter your **bank account details**:

| Field | What to Enter |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Bank Name** | e.g., Commercial Bank of Ethiopia |
| **Account Holder Name** | Pharmacy owner's name |
| **Account Number** | Your bank account number |

3. Upload a **voided check** or **bank letter** (if required)
4. Tap **"Submit for Verification"**

> ⏳ Verification takes 2-3 business days.

---

## 🔐 Security Settings

### Change Your Password

1. Go to **Profile** → **"Security"**
2. Tap **"Change Password"**
3. Enter:
   - Current password
   - New password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Confirm new password
4. Tap **"Update Password"**

### Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

For extra security:

1. Tap **"Enable 2FA"**
2. Scan the QR code with Google Authenticator
3. Enter the 6-digit code
4. Tap **"Verify"**

> 🔒 After enabling, you will need a code from your authenticator app to log in.

---

## 🔔 Notification Settings

Choose how you want to be alerted:

| Notification Type | Options |
| :--- | :--- |
| **New Orders** | Push notification, Email, Both, Off |
| **Payment Received** | Push notification, Email, Both, Off |
| **Low Stock Alerts** | Push notification, Email, Both, Off |
| **Marketing Updates** | Email only, Off |

**How to change:**
1. Go to **Profile** → **"Notifications"**
2. Toggle each option ON/OFF
3. Changes save automatically

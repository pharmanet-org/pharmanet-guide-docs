# 📋 Process Incoming Orders

Manage customer orders quickly and efficiently. This guide shows you how to track, update, and complete orders from your pharmacy.

---

## 🖥️ Where to Find Orders

After logging into your **Pharmacist Dashboard**:

1. Look at the **left sidebar** or **bottom navigation bar**
2. Tap on **"Orders"** or **"Order Management"**
3. You will see a list of all customer orders

---

## 📊 Understanding Order Status

Orders move through these stages:

| Status | What It Means | Your Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| ⏳ **Pending** | Customer placed order, waiting for pharmacy | Review and confirm |
| ✅ **Confirmed** | You accepted the order | Prepare medicines |
| 🚚 **Shipped** | Order handed to delivery partner | Share tracking info |
| 📦 **Delivered** | Customer received items | Mark as complete |
| ❌ **Cancelled** | Order was cancelled | No action needed |

---

## 🆕 View New Orders

When a customer places an order:

1. You will see a **red badge** on the Orders icon
2. Tap **"Orders"** to view the list
3. New orders appear at the **top** with "Pending" status

## ✅ Confirm an Order

1. Tap **"View Details"** on the order
2. Review the customer's items and delivery address
3. Check your **inventory** to ensure all items are in stock
4. Tap the **"Confirm Order"** button
5. Status changes from ⏳ Pending → ✅ Confirmed

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Confirm orders within 30 minutes for best customer satisfaction.

---

## 📝 Update Order Status

As you fulfill the order, keep the customer informed:

### Step 1: Prepare Items
- Collect all medicines from your inventory
- Double-check quantities and expiry dates
- Pack securely

### Step 2: Mark as "Ready for Pickup" or "Shipped"

| Delivery Method | What to do |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Customer picks up** | Tap "Ready for Pickup" → customer gets notification |
| **Delivery partner** | Tap "Mark as Shipped" → enter tracking number (if available) |

### Step 3: Complete the Order
- After customer receives items, tap **"Mark as Delivered"**
- Order moves to completed history

---

## ❌ Decline or Cancel an Order

Only decline if you **cannot fulfill** the order:

**Valid reasons:**
- Medicine out of stock
- Customer requested cancellation
- Payment verification failed

**How to decline:**
1. Open order details
2. Tap **"Decline Order"** or **"Cancel"**
3. Select a reason from the dropdown
4. Add a short note for the customer (optional)
5. Tap **"Confirm Cancellation"**

> ⚠️ The customer will be notified immediately and refunded if payment was made.

## 🔍 Search & Filter Orders

Use the **search bar** and **filters** to find specific orders:

| Filter | What it shows |
| :--- | :--- |
| **All Orders** | Complete history |
| **Pending** | Orders waiting for you |
| **Confirmed** | Orders you accepted |
| **Shipped** | Orders in transit |
| **Delivered** | Completed orders |
| **Cancelled** | Declined orders |

**Search by:**
- Order number (e.g., #ORD-1234)
- Customer name
- Date range

## ⚠️ Troubleshooting

| Problem | What to do |
| :--- | :--- |
| Order not showing up | Refresh the page (pull down to reload) |
| Can't confirm order | Check if items are in stock |
| Wrong status displayed | Tap "Refresh" or restart the app |
| Customer says not delivered | Verify tracking info and contact delivery partner |
| Payment status stuck | Ask customer to share payment receipt via chat |

---

## 💬 Communicate with Customers

Use the **Chat** feature to:
- Confirm order details
- Share payment instructions
- Update delivery status
- Resolve issues

**How to chat:**
1. Open order details
2. Tap **"Message Customer"** (speech bubble icon)
3. Type your message
4. Tap send

---

## ✅ Order Completion Checklist

Before marking an order as **Delivered**, confirm:

- [ ] All items picked and packed
- [ ] Quantities match the order
- [ ] Expiry dates are valid (at least 3 months remaining)
- [ ] Payment received (if cash on delivery, payment collected)
- [ ] Customer confirmed receipt

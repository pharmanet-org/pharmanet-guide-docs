# Account & Login Guide

## Three Types of PharmaNet Users

| Role | Who it's for | Main purpose |
|------|--------------|--------------|
| **Seller (Pharmacy)** | Pharmacy owners and managers | List products, manage inventory, accept orders |
| **Dealer (Distributor)** | Wholesalers and distributors | Search products, place orders, negotiate pricing |
| **Public User** | General public, researchers, visitors | View platform info, search public catalogs |

---

## Creating an Account (Sign Up)

### Step 1: Choose Your Role
On the signup screen, select one of three roles:

| Role | Selection |
|------|-----------|
| **Seller** | Tap "Sign up as Seller" |
| **Dealer** | Tap "Sign up as Dealer" |
| **Public User** | Tap "Sign up as Public User" |

---

### Step 2: Fill Registration Form (All Roles)

All users must enter:

| Field | What to enter |
|-------|---------------|
| **Email** | Your email address |
| **Password** | Create a secure password |
| **Confirm Password** | Type the same password again |

Tap **Sign Up** to continue.

---

### Alternative: Sign Up with Google or Apple (All Roles)

Instead of email/password, you can sign up using:

| Method | How to use |
|--------|------------|
| **Google** | Tap the Google button → Select your Google account |
| **Apple** | Tap the Apple button → Authenticate with Face ID/Touch ID |

> ✅ Benefits: Faster registration, no password to remember.

---

## Role-Specific Information Required

After basic signup, each role must provide additional information:

---

### 👨‍⚕️ Seller (Pharmacy) – Additional Requirements

| Field | What to provide |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Pharmacy Name** | Official business name |
| **Pharmacy License Number** | Government-issued license |
| **Tax ID (TIN)** | Tax identification number |
| **Physical Address** | Complete pharmacy location |
| **Phone Number** | Business contact number |

**Documents to upload:**
- ✅ Pharmacy license (PDF or image)
- ✅ Tax certificate
- ✅ Business registration

> ⏱ **Verification time:** 24-48 hours

---

### 🚚 Dealer (Distributor) – Additional Requirements

| Field | What to provide |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Distributor Name** | Official business name |
| **Wholesale License Number** | Government-issued wholesale license |
| **Tax ID (TIN)** | Tax identification number |
| **Warehouse Address** | Storage facility location |
| **Phone Number** | Business contact number |
| **Service Areas** | Regions/cities you deliver to |

**Documents to upload:**
- ✅ Wholesale license (PDF or image)
- ✅ Tax certificate
- ✅ Business registration
- ✅ Warehouse proof (optional)

> ⏱ **Verification time:** 24-48 hours

---

### 👤 Public User – Additional Requirements

| Field | What to provide |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Full Name** | Your real name |
| **Phone Number (optional)** | For notifications |

**Documents required:**
- ❌ No documents needed

> ⚡ **Verification:** Instant – no waiting period

## Logging In (All Roles)

You have **three ways** to log in to PharmaNet:

### Option 1: Email & Password Login
1. On the welcome screen, tap **Login**
2. Enter your **Email**
3. Enter your **Password**
4. Tap the login button

### Option 2: Sign in with Google
1. Tap the **Google** button
2. Select your Google account
3. Automatically logged in

### Option 3: Sign in with Apple
1. Tap the **Apple** button
2. Authenticate with Face ID/Touch ID
3. Automatically logged in

## Forgot Password? (Email Login Only)

1. On the **Login** screen, tap **"Forgot Password?"**
2. Enter your registered email
3. Check your email for reset link
4. Create a new password
5. Log in with your new password

> ⚠️ **Google/Apple users:** You don't have a PharmaNet password. Use the Google or Apple button to log in.

## Common Signup Issues by Role

### For Sellers & Dealers

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| License upload fails | File must be PDF or JPG/PNG, max 5MB |
| Verification takes >3 days | Contact admin@pharmanet.bilsul.com |
| "License number invalid" | Double-check the number matches your document |

### For Public Users

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Email already registered | You may have signed up as Seller/Dealer before. Try logging in. |
| Can't see full catalog | Public users see limited products. Upgrade to Seller or Dealer for full access. |

### For All Roles

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| "Passwords do not match" | Re-type both password fields |
| "Invalid email format" | Must contain @ and domain (e.g., name@domain.com) |
| No verification email | Check spam folder, or wait 2-3 minutes |

## Switching Roles

| Current Role | Can you switch? | How |
|--------------|----------------|-----|
| Public User → Seller | ✅ Yes | Contact support to upgrade |
| Public User → Dealer | ✅ Yes | Contact support to upgrade |
| Seller → Dealer | ❌ No | Create separate Dealer account |
| Dealer → Seller | ❌ No | Create separate Seller account |

> 💡 **Tip:** You can have both a Seller and Dealer account using different email addresses.

## Security Tips (All Roles)

| Do ✅ | Don't ❌ |
|-------|----------|
| Use a unique password | Share your password with anyone |
| Log out on shared devices | Save password on public computers |
| Enable email notifications | Use obvious passwords (password123) |
| Use Google/Apple sign up for extra security | Click login links from unknown emails |
